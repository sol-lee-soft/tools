System Methodology & Data Integrity

Calculations provided by this suite adhere to standard financial and clinical logic. Discrepancies between this software and external banking or clinical systems typically arise from differing assumptions in compounding frequency, day-count conventions, or rounding steps.

  • Floating-Point Precision: All background logic is processed using high-precision 64-bit floating-point math.
  • Final-Step Rounding: To prevent "rounding drift" across multi-step formulas, outputs are rounded (to the nearest cent or decimal) only at the final display stage.

1. Financial Logic & Formulas

A. Growth & Compounding

Assumes a standard 365-day year and 12 compounding periods per year.

Future Value (A) = P * (1 + r/n)^(nt)

B. Universal Finance (Amortization)

Solves for the Monthly Payment (PMT) of a fixed-term, fixed-rate loan.

PMT = P * (r * (1 + r)^n) / ((1 + r)^n - 1)

C. Inflation & Purchasing Power

Future projections decay the value exponentially against the target rate.

Real Value = Target Payout / (1 + r)^Years

2. Clinical Metrics (The 8 Diagnostic Tools)

Hepatology and Renal formulas are mapped to validated physiological constants.

1. eGFR (CKD-EPI 2021)

The current race-neutral standard for estimating glomerular filtration rate.

eGFR = 142 * min(Cr/k, 1)^a * max(Cr/k, 1)^-1.2 * 0.9938^Age * [1.012 if female]

2. BUN/Creatinine Ratio

Used to assess the etiology of acute kidney injury or dehydration.

Ratio = Blood Urea Nitrogen (BUN) / Serum Creatinine

3. Maddrey Discriminant Function (mDF)

Assesses the severity and mortality risk of alcoholic hepatitis.

mDF = 4.6 * (Patient PT - Control PT) + Total Bilirubin

4. FIB-4 Index

A non-invasive scoring system for estimating liver fibrosis.

FIB-4 = (Age * AST) / (Platelets * √ALT)

5. MELD (Original)

The baseline Model for End-Stage Liver Disease.

MELD = 3.78*ln(Bilirubin) + 11.2*ln(INR) + 9.57*ln(Creatinine) + 6.43

6. MELD-Na

The sodium-adjusted evolution of the MELD score.

MELD-Na = MELD + 1.32*(137-Na) - [0.033*MELD*(137-Na)]

7. MELD 3.0

The updated 2023 scoring model accounting for albumin and sex disparities.

MELD 3.0 = 1.33(if female) + 4.56*ln(Bili) + 0.82(137-Na) - 0.24(137-Na)*ln(Bili) + 9.09*ln(INR) + 11.14*ln(Cr) + 1.85(3.5-Alb) - 1.83(3.5-Alb)*ln(Cr) + 6

8. Child-Pugh Score

A clinical summation (Class A, B, or C) where patients receive 1 to 3 points in five categories:

  • Total Bilirubin: <2 (1pt) | 2-3 (2pts) | >3 (3pts)
  • Serum Albumin: >3.5 (1pt) | 2.8-3.5 (2pts) | <2.8 (3pts)
  • INR: <1.7 (1pt) | 1.7-2.3 (2pts) | >2.3 (3pts)
  • Ascites: None (1pt) | Mild (2pts) | Severe (3pts)
  • Encephalopathy: None (1pt) | Grade I-II (2pts) | Grade III-IV (3pts)
